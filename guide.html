Rotation

Opener

Pre Pull
  • Stand 25 yards away, use Potion of the Jade Serpent at 2.5s and cast Halo
  • Cast Mind Spike
  1. Cast Shadowfiend
  2. Cast Berserking and Synapse Springs
  3. Cast Shadow Word: Pain
  4. Cast Vampiric Touch
  5. Cast Devouring Plague
  6. Cast Mind Blast
  7. Cast Mind Flay: Insanity

Interrupt and recast Shadow Word: Pain and Vampiric Touch if Unerring Vision of Lei Shen procs.

Spell Priority (highest → lowest)

  1. Devouring Plague — 3 Shadow Orbs only.
  2. Mind Blast — Use on cooldown.
  3. Shadow Word: Death — Use both charges on cooldown.
  4. Mind Flay: Insanity — Recast just before Devouring Plague expires to snapshot the channel.
  5. Shadow Word: Pain / Vampiric Touch — Aim for 100% uptime.
  6. Mind Spike — Only with From Darkness, Comes Light.
  7. Halo — Use on cooldown with proper positioning (20+ yards).
  8. Mind Flay — Interrupt early for higher priorities.
Before casting Devouring Plague, make sure Shadow Word: Pain and Vampiric Touch both have at least 8 seconds remaining — so you won't need to interrupt Mind Flay: Insanity to refresh them.

Macros

CDs

#showtooltip
/cast Berserking
/cast Power Infusion
/use 10
/use 13
/use 14

Pops all our major cooldowns at once.
/use 10 — Gloves (Synapse Springs) /use 13 — Trinket 1 /use 14 — Trinket 2

Shadowfiend

#showtooltip
/cast Shadowfiend
/cleartarget [dead][noharm]
/targetenemy [noexists]
/petattack

Casts Shadowfiend and forces it to attack your target.

SWD

#showtooltip Shadow Word: Death
/cleartarget [dead][noharm]
/targetenemy [noexists]
/cast [stance] Shadow Word: Death; [nostance] Shadowform
/cast Shadow Word: Death

Ensures you're in Shadowform before casting Shadow Word: Death. Automatically targets a new enemy if your current target is dead — useful for sniping low-health adds like Ball Lightning.

MOP

#showtooltip
/cast [talent:6/1]Cascade
/cast [talent:6/2]Divine Star
/cast [talent:6/3]Halo

Automatically uses whichever Level 90 talent you have selected — Cascade, Divine Star, or Halo — with the correct tooltip.

Dispersion

#showtooltip
/cast Dispersion
/cancelaura Dispersion

Press once to activate Dispersion, press again to cancel.

Feather

#showtooltip
/cast [@player] Angelic Feather

Automatically places Angelic Feather at your feet.
If stacked in a group, step slightly away from others to make sure you still receive it.
