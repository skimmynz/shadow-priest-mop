Shadow Priest Guide — Mists of Pandaria Classic

Talents

15
Void Tendrils

Roots adds in place — useful for Council add spawns and Lei Shen's Diffusion Chain adds.

30
Angelic Feather

80% movement speed on a 10 second cooldown with 3 charges. Compare against retail version.

45
Solace and Insanity

Strongest single-target option. Swap to Mindbender on Council and Primordius.

60
Angelic Bulwark

Passive shield that procs at low health — set and forget.

75
Twist of Fate

15% damage buff when damaging targets below 35% HP. Snipe low-health adds with Shadow Word: Pain and Shadow Word: Death to maintain high uptime.

90
Halo

Strongest option if you can stay at 20+ yard range. Switch to Cascade on Primordius. Switch to Divine Star on Council and Ra-den (also Megaera if healers need help).

For boss-specific talent swaps, see the T15 Tips tab.

FAQ

  • Placeholder — What's the best pre-pot for Shadow Priest? Jade Serpent Potion, used 1 second before pull.
  • Placeholder — Should I use Dispersion on cooldown? Use it during movement phases to regenerate mana without losing uptime.
  • Placeholder — What DoT uptime should I aim for? 95%+ on SW:P and VT — track with WeakAuras.
  • Placeholder — Multi-dot padding vs priority damage? Communicate with your raid leader on fight-by-fight basis.

T15 Tips

  • Placeholder — Jin'rokh: Stand in pools for damage buff, multi-dot Focused Lightning orbs.
  • Placeholder — Horridon: Multi-dot add waves aggressively, save cooldowns for Door 3 and Door 4.
  • Placeholder — Council of Elders: Maintain DoTs on all 4 bosses, priority damage on empowered target.
  • Placeholder — Megaera: Spread DoTs across all heads, use Halo for maximum AoE value.
