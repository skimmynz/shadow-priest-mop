Rotation

Opener

Pre Pull
  • Stand 25 yards away, use Potion of the Jade Serpent at 2.5s and cast Halo
  • Cast Mind Spike
  1. Cast Shadowfiend
  2. Cast Berserking and Synapse Springs
  3. Cast Shadow Word: Pain
  4. Cast Vampiric Touch
  5. Cast Devouring Plague
  6. Cast Mind Blast
  7. Cast Mind Flay: Insanity

Interrupt and recast Shadow Word: Pain and Vampiric Touch if Unerring Vision of Lei Shen procs.

Spell Priority (highest → lowest)

  1. Devouring Plague — 3 Shadow Orbs only.
  2. Mind Blast — Use on cooldown.
  3. Shadow Word: Death — Use both charges on cooldown.
  4. Mind Flay: Insanity — Recast just before Devouring Plague expires to snapshot the channel.
  5. Shadow Word: Pain / Vampiric Touch — Aim for 100% uptime.
  6. Mind Spike — Only with From Darkness, Comes Light.
  7. Halo — Use on cooldown with proper positioning (20+ yards).
  8. Mind Flay — Interrupt early for higher priorities.
Before casting Devouring Plague, make sure Shadow Word: Pain and Vampiric Touch both have at least 8 seconds remaining — so you won't need to interrupt Mind Flay: Insanity to refresh them.

T15 Tips

  • Prepot Potion of the Jade Serpent at 15s and hold your cooldowns.
  • With Bloodlust and Fluidity, use your CDs and 2nd Potion of the Jade Serpent. RNG Unerring Vision of Lei Shen procs.
  • After you clear Ionization, get back into the Fluidity pool quickly and reapply DoTs.
  • Use Vampiric Embrace during Lightning Storm.
  • Apply Shadow Word: Pain to small adds. Keep Shadow Word: Pain and Vampiric Touch on marked elite adds.
  • Double DoT the Direhorn Spirit to benefit from the Tier 15 Shadow Priest 2P bonus.
  • Spend Devouring Plague and Mind Flay: Insanity on Horridon.
  • Use Vampiric Embrace during high Bestial Cry stacks.

Horridon is removed from Damage All Star Points.

  • Stack in melee so you can hit multiple targets with Divine Star on cooldown.
  • Keep DoTs rolling on all bosses and fill with Mind Sear.
  • Recast DoTs when Unerring Vision of Lei Shen procs.

Damage done to Living Sand is removed.

  • Cast Shadow Word: Pain and Shadow Word: Death on adds to trigger Twist of Fate and generate Shadow Orbs.
  • Maintain Shadow Word: Pain on a Humming Crystal for the shield.
  • Stay spread to reduce the chance of being knocked up by the Whirl Turtles.

Damage done to Humming Crystal is removed. Heroic: Damage done to Vampiric Cave Bat is removed. Only damage done to Whirl Turtles that cast Shell Concussion counts.

  • Ignore the Venomous Head.
  • Cast Shadow Word: Death on Nether Wyrms to generate Shadow Orbs.
  • Swap to Divine Star if your raid needs extra healing throughput.

Damage done to heads that don't die is removed. 25m Heroic: Damage done to Nether Wyrm is removed.

  • Use feather charges to intercept Feed Young and gain Primal Nutriment.
  • Cast DoTs on the Nest Guardian. Use Mind Sear and Shadow Word: Death otherwise.
  • Use Vampiric Embrace during Quills if raid is low.

Ji-Kun is removed from Damage All Star Points.

  • Snipe Crimson Fog adds with Shadow Word: Death for Twist of Fate uptime and Shadow Orbs.
  • Cast Halo and DoTs on the Ice Walls.

Damage done to Wandering Eye is removed.

  • Keep DoTs on all Living Fluids (unless your Warlocks know how to play Affliction).
  • Use Cascade on cooldown.
  • Adjust your camera angle to help dodge puddles and avoid unwanted debuffs.

Primordius is removed from Damage and Healing All Star Points.

  • DoT as many Anima Golems as possible to maintain Twist of Fate.
  • Use Cascade on cooldown for efficient multi-target damage.
  • Focus your Devouring Plague and Mind Flay: Insanity into the boss.

Heroic: Damage to Large Anima Golem and Massive Anima Golem is removed. Only count damage done to Anima Golems that die.

  • Recast DoTs on Iron Qon during mount transitions (Ro'shak → Quet'zal → Dam'ren).
  • Hold your 2nd cooldowns and Potion of the Jade Serpent for the final phase, when all four targets are active.
  • Use Vampiric Embrace during Fist Smash if raid is low.

Damage done to Ice Tomb is removed.

  • Precast Vampiric Touch on Suen then Shadow Word: Pain before she vanishes.
  • Refresh DoTs on Suen during Tears of the Sun.
  • During Nuclear Inferno (boss immune), focus on off-healing.
  • Use Vampiric Embrace when Blazing Radiance stacks get high.

Damage done to Lurker in the Night is removed.

  • Use Halo and Mind Sear to handle Ball Lightnings.
  • Snipe low adds with Shadow Word: Death to generate Shadow Orbs.
  • Save your CDs and 2nd Potion of the Jade Serpent for Bloodlust.

Damage done to Unharnessed Power, Lesser Diffused Lightning, Greater Diffused Lightning, and Diffused Lightning is removed.

  • DoT the Crackling Stalker, then burst it with Divine Star and Shadow Word: Death.
  • Snipe the first Essence of Anima with Shadow Word: Death. Burn the remaining ones with Devouring Plague and Mind Flay: Insanity.
  • Sub 40%, use Divine Star on cooldown to help with the HPS check.

Damage done to Sanguine Horror, Corrupted Anima, Corrupted Vita, and Essence of Vita is removed.
